Eye Wear Trends

Oversized glasses: Make a statement

Oversized glasses are a bold trend for eyewear in 2023 and perfect for anyone who wants to make a statement with their fashion. These statement glasses add a touch of chic to any outfit and come in many different shapes and sizes. They are perfect for anyone who wants to add a touch of glamour and confidence to their look. Look for brands like CUTLER AND GROSS that have engrained boldness into their DNA.

Sustainable eyewear: Eco-friendly and fashionable

Sustainability is a major trend for eyewear this year, and at FAVR we offer a wide selection of eco-friendly and sustainable eyewear made from recycled or renewable materials like bamboo or wood. These glasses not only look stylish and fashionable, but also help protect the environment. If you’re looking for an accessory that’s both fashionable and eco-friendly, sustainable eyewear is a great choice for you

Frameless eyewear: Elegant and minimalist

Frameless glasses are the perfect accessory for those who prefer a minimalist look, and are one of the top trends for eyewear in 2023. With their clean lines and understated style, these glasses add a touch of sophistication and simplicity to any outfit, making them perfect for both casual and formal occasions. If you’re looking for a sleek, minimalist accessory that won’t make your look overbearing, frameless glasses are just what you need – that’s why they’re among the most important eyewear trends

Filigree eyeglass frames: timeless elegance

Thin, filigree frames are a timeless, classic trend for eyewear in 2023 and add a touch of elegance to any outfit. Filigree frames, in particular, are back in style in 2023. These frames are made of thin metal wire twisted into intricate patterns, giving them a unique and elegant look. They are perfect for those who want to add a touch of sophistication to their eyewear collection.

Natural materials: stylish and eco-friendly

Eyewear trends for this year focus on sustainability and eco-friendliness. At FAVR, we offer a range of eyewear made from natural materials like wood or bamboo, giving you a stylish and eco-friendly accessory. Not only do these glasses look great, but they also help reduce your carbon footprint. If you’re looking for glasses that are both stylish and sustainable, glasses made from natural materials are a great choice.

3D-printed eyewear: Customizable and unique

3D printing is a new trend for eyewear in 2023, and at FAVR we offer a range of 3D printed eyewear that is unique and customizable. Our 3D printed eyewear is not only stylish and modern, but also sustainable as it uses less material and energy compared to traditional manufacturing processes. In addition, 3D printing allows for intricate designs that are not possible with traditional manufacturing methods, making them a trendy and unique choice for eyewear in 2023.
